Schedule

Preschool

The school begins at 9:45am and closes at 3:00pm.

  • The classes start with morning meeting of 10 to 20 minutes. During the morning meeting students take turn to read aloud morning message of the day which leads to class discussions.

    • The morning meeting is led by action rhymes and developmental games
  • After the above rituals, the children engage in intensive theme based experiential activities.
  • They get snack break from 12:30 to 1:10
  • Lower KG and upper KG have separate periods for language and math lessons. Nursery has only 30 minutes each day allocated for teacher led lessons.
  • The students prepare to get ready for home from 2:30 to 2:45.
  • The class-teachers peacefully read aloud to them before they leave class at 3:00.

Early Primary ( Grades 1 to 3)

The school begins at 9:45am and closes at 3:30pm – After the 15 minutes assembly classes start with morning meeting from 10:00 to 10:20. The students take turn to read message/question of the day. This leads to discussion on that day’s lead activity. – After the morning meeting students engage in various theme based activities from 10:20 to 11:00. – There are separate periods for languages ( Nepali and English) and Math. – They get snack break from 1:10 to 2:00. – The students get ready for departure at 3:10. – The class-teachers reads aloud to them before they leave classroom at 3:30

Upper Primary ( Grades 4 to 8)

The school begins at 9:45 am and closes at 4:00 pm – After the 15 minutes assembly classes start with morning meeting from 10:00 to 10:20. The students take turn to read message/question of the day. This leads to discussion on that day’s lead activity on Science or Social Studies. – They get snack break from 1:10 to 2:00. – Grades 4 and 5 get three periods in a week for free play. – Grades 4 and 5 have flexible lesson schedules that can vary from day to day while grades 6 and upwards have fixed one hour twenty minutes periods allocated for each subjects on alternative basis.
